How It Works

Step 01

Browse the Pokédex

Explore all 151 original Pokémon with full base stats, abilities, height, weight, and type information fetched live from PokéAPI.

Step 02

Build Your Team

Add up to six Pokémon to your saved roster directly from the Pokédex. Your team is stored locally so it's always waiting for you on your next visit.

Step 03

Analyze Coverage

Head to My Team to review shared type weaknesses and your offensive coverage. Fine-tune your lineup to plug any strategic gaps before battle.
