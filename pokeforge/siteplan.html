PokéForge

Website Site Plan — Web Development Course Project

01 Site Name

Name
PokéForge

"PokéForge" combines the iconic Pokémon brand prefix with "Forge," suggesting the act of crafting and building something powerful. Just as a blacksmith forges tools, users forge their ideal battle team. The name is short, memorable, and clearly communicates the site's purpose.

Potential Domain
pokeforge.app

02 Site Purpose

PokéForge is an interactive Pokémon Team Builder that lets fans browse the full Pokédex, explore individual Pokémon stats, and assemble a saved team of up to six Pokémon. The site pulls live data from the free PokéAPI and persists the user's team using local storage so progress is never lost between visits.

Beyond browsing, the site provides a team analysis tool that evaluates type coverage — identifying offensive strengths and defensive weaknesses in the selected team — helping players make more strategic choices.

03 Scenarios

Questions a target visitor might ask when coming to the site:

  • "I want to build a balanced team — how do I know if my team has too many weaknesses to Water-type moves?"
  • "Which Pokémon of the Fire type has the highest base Speed stat so I can outspeed my opponent?"
  • "I built a team last week — can I come back to the site and see it again without re-adding everything?"
  • "What are all the abilities and stats for Gengar before I decide whether to add it to my team?"

05 Typography

Font 1 — Exo 2 · Headings, Site Name, Navigation, Buttons

PokéForge Team Builder

Bold, geometric, and futuristic. Used for all headings (h1–h3), the site name, nav links, and UI labels. Weights: 700 and 900.

Font 2 — Nunito · Body Text, Descriptions, Card Content

Bulbasaur is a Grass/Poison-type Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It has a base stat total of 318 and is known for its resilient defensive typing.

Rounded and approachable. Used for all body copy, Pokémon descriptions, and form labels. Weight: 400 and 600.

Font 2 (variant) — Exo 2 · UI Labels, Stat Tags, Type Badges

Base Stat Total  ·  Fire Type  ·  Speed: 120

Exo 2 at small size with wide letter-spacing for data labels and type badges throughout the UI.
