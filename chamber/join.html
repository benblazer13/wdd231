NP Membership Benefits

Fee waiver, dedicated nonprofit support line, invitations to volunteer-project planning and community impact forums.

Perks include discounted event sponsorships, partner referral promotions, and nonprofit-specific outreach campaigns.

Bronze Membership Benefits

Business listing in directory, invitation to monthly mixers, and access to member newsletter.

Includes one complimentary event admission, startup guidance, and basic social spotlight placement.

Silver Membership Benefits

All Bronze benefits plus up to three featured event sponsorships, quarterly business training, and profile advertising.

Priority placement in member spotlights and access to discounted professional services partners.

Gold Membership Benefits

All Silver benefits plus premium homepage spotlight, leadership roundtables, and exclusive large sponsor placement.

Includes enhanced marketing, priority event booth space and custom training bundle across the year.
